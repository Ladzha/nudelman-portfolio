Avatar creator

The project came to life during a hackathon within a Coding Bootcamp. I conceptualized an Avatar Creator that empowers users to design their unique avatars by mixing and matching various facial components. The process starts with selecting a face shape, followed by choosing from a set of hairstyles. This step-by-step approach allows users to craft avatars that suit their preferences. The illustrations are crafted digitally, adding a distinct touch to each avatar. Users can preserve their creations in the gallery, complete with personalized names, for convenient viewing in the future.
